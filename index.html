Siren Watcher's Followed Actors

Actors that, if are in a show, I am more likely to watch it.

James Spader

Favourites:

Boston Legal, Blacklist

Spader is amazing as an actor, I love the witty analytical (disagreeable) nature of all his characters.

Catherine Zeta-Jones

Favourites:

Intolerable Cruelty, The Mask of Zorro

It's just basic attraction to be honest, she has been my favourite actress since I was about 12. I love the snarky combatitive nature of her characters.

Johnny Depp

Favourites:

Secret Window, Fear and Loathing in Las Vegas

I always enjoyed Depps eccentricities and creativity in his performances. I love the creative extremes, the 'oddness', of his roles.

Selma Hayek

Favourites:

Once Upon a Time in Mexico, Dogma

I like Selma's sassiness, I like the roles she chooses.

Hugo Weaving

Favourites:

V for Vendetta, Matrix Revolutions (why three: the architect concept and the world between worlds)

I like the depth Hugo goes into his characters, the air of seriousness and immovability of that seriousness to his portrayals.

Robert Downey Jr.

Favourites:

Sherlock Holmes: A Game of Shadows, Oppenheimer

I like Downey Jr, I like that all his characters are arrogant, it calms me.

Natasha Lyonne

Favourites:

Russian Doll, American Pie

I actually always liked Lyonne's characters, as far back as American Pie when I was a teen. Whatever I tend to watch with her in it I enjoy her character portrayals just based on personality similarity (hyper open and assertive).

Jared Leto

Favourites:

Requiem for a Dream, Lord of War

I actually saw Leto's band play live when I was a teen, he portrays oddness well. As in, when you look at a piece of art that draws you in but also seeks to disorientate you at the same time through creative variance.

Edward Norton

Favourites:

Fight Club, The Illusionist

I feel like Norton is someone I am not meant to like personally, but I adore him. I like his 'emotional breaks', how grounded he tries to maintain in his characters and then the breakout of emotion.

Richard Roxburgh

Favourites:

Rake, Van Helsing

I like Roxburgh, he shows up as side characters in a lot of things, I adored him in Rake.

Aidan Gallagher

Favourites:

Umbrella Academy

Five is an amazing portrayed character, he split the show somewhat into differentiated skill levels of acting.
